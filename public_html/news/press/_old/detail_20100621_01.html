[Live streaming] Next 30-Year Vision

Jun. 21,2010
SoftBank Group Corp.

SOFTBANK CORP. (hereafter “the Company”) will present Next 30-Year Vision to shareholders and separately invited general public after the end of the 30th Annual General Meeting of Shareholders on June 25, 2010.

One thousand people chosen by lottery will be invited to this presentation which is a part of 30th anniversary event of the Company.

Masayoshi Son, Chairman and CEO of the SOFTBANK Group will present the vision of the SOFTBANK Group for the next 30 years.

Details are as follows:

1. Date, Time and Venue

Date and timeAfter the end of the Annual General Meeting of Shareholders on June 25, 2010 (Registration start at 10:30am)
[Note]
  • *Presentation will start after 15 minutes break following the Annual General Meeting of Shareholders. The Annual General Meeting of Shareholders will start at 10:00 AM, June 25, 2010.
VenueHall A, Tokyo International Forum
(5-1, Marunouchi 3-chome, Chiyoda-ku, Tokyo)

2. Agenda

Masayoshi Son, Chairman and CEO of the SOFTBANK Group will explain the vision of the SOFTBANK Group for the next 30 years.
